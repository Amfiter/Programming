Dorian made no answer, but passed listlessly in front of his picture and turned towards it.
When he saw it he drew back, and his cheeks flushed 
for a moment with pleasure. A look of joy came into his eyes,
as if he had recognised himself for the first time.
He stood there motionless and in wonder, dimly conscious that Hallward was speaking to him,
but not catching the meaning of his words.
